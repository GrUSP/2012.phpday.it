New speaker confirmations and JetBrains Platinum sponsorship

We are pleased to announce a few more speakers for the phpDay conference, congratulations to:

Filippo Desantis – Building a self-sufficient team

Stefano Maraspin – Get’em in shape: let customers appreciate the agile workflow

Giorgio Sironi – What they didn’t tell you about object-oriented programming in PHP

Paolo Mainardi & Stefano Mainardi – Mobile Drupal

Helgi Thorbjoernsson – PHAR, the PHP .exe format

There might still be a few slots available and, if so, we will assign them shortly.
Please bear with us, but we’re still waiting for some confirmations from other speakers and sponsors.

We are pleased to announce another big sponsor: JetBrains!
We are also happy to announce the new media partnership with PhpBenelux, the PHP usergroup for the Benelux that every year organize the great PhpBenelux conference.

Remember that the early bird tickets sale ends in a few days, April 1st, hurry up!
In the next days we are going to publish the schedule, meanwhile check the great speakers list

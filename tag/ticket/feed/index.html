GrUSP is organising a tweet contest and its prizes will be two tickets, one for each conference. The contest will start today (March 29th) at 1pm CEST and will end April 1st at 7pm CEST.

To enter, just post a tweet containing:

  • the #grusp hashtag
  • a mention to either @jsconfit or @phpday
  • the corresponding link, https://2012.jsday.it or https://2012.phpday.it
  • something about the event that actually makes sense

 

On Sunday april 1st at 9pm CEST we will randomly pick a winner for each conference among all the tweets matching the criteria above.

Multiple entries from the same author are accepted, which means that the more you tweet about us, the bigger your chances to win will be!

If you already bought a ticket (or you are thinking to do so while early bird tickets are available), don’t worry! If you happen to be one of the lucky winners, the ticket cost will be refunded!
